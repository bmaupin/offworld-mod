# In-game music

#### Songs

- The in-game music is split across 6 "songs" (A - F)
- A song is randomly picked and used for each match

#### Tracks

- Each song is split into 15 different tracks
- Track 1 loops when the map is shown before the HQ is founded
- The even tracks are played once at different events in the game; they serve as transition tracks
  - 2: HQ founded
  - 4: HQ 2
  - 6: HQ 3
  - 8: HQ 4
  - 10: HQ 5
  - 12: Victory
  - 14: Loss
- The remaining odd tracks are played in a loop after the even tracks until the next event triggers another even track
